Core Challenges of SEM Projects

Traffic Without Conversion

Keywords and creatives bring clicks, but page content, trust signals, and form paths fail to capture real needs.

Data Without Judgment

Do not look only at clicks and spend; also identify which terms, regions, pages, and language versions bring truly effective leads.

Ads Without a System

Cross-border and domestic businesses involve different platforms and search habits, so accounts, pages, and review mechanisms need to work together.

Optimization Loop

Sustained conversions do not come from one-time bid adjustments; they come from continuously optimizing account diagnostics, keyword hierarchy, ad creative, landing pages, and conversion tracking together.

01

Account Diagnosis

Analyze account structure, search terms, spend, regional performance, and historical conversions.

02

Strategy Breakdown

Choose suitable platforms and campaign structures based on business, region, language, and budget.

03

Keywords and Creative

Segment keywords by search intent and optimize ad copy and negative keyword strategy.

04

Landing Page Support

Optimize service pages, FAQs, cases, forms, and trust information together.

05

Data Review

Continuously adjust budgets, keyword sets, pages, and inquiry paths based on conversion quality.

Service Advantages

Start with Market and Demand

Instead of applying fixed templates, first determine where customers are, what language they search in, and what terms they use.

Unify Keywords, Creative, and Pages

Ad copy is only the entry point; conversion is shaped by page messaging, trust content, and action paths.

Focus on Lead Quality

Rather than only pursuing form volume, focus on whether inquiries are accurate and easier to convert later.

Cover Overseas and Domestic Markets

Foreign trade independent sites can focus on Google Ads and multilingual pages, while domestic business can combine Chinese search platform optimization.

Make Ad Budgets Closer to Real Business Results

If you are running ads but conversions are unstable, start by diagnosing both the account and landing pages.
